Jessica Hunter, photographer & artist

    I am an art student in Year 12 at Diocesan School for Girls in Auckland, New Zealand.

    I am a passionate artist and enjoy photography, fine art printing and drawing.

    I believe that not only art out freedom to express ourselves, but it is also a way to capture moments of happiness, of celebration to share with the people in the world of today and tomorrow. People will pass away, fade into the pages of our history books, but art is timeless.
